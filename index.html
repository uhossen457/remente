Professor Antonios Dakanalis, Remente’s Scientific Ambassador

We are thrilled to announce Professor Antonios Dakanalis as Remente as our Scientific Ambassador. Professor Dakanalis, a leading figure in Psychiatry and Psychotherapy at the University of Milan, is recognized among the top 50 scientists worldwide in mental health. His pioneering work leverages cutting-edge technology, such as Virtual Reality and Artificial Intelligence, to transform mental health services.

As the director of multiple prestigious research centers and a prolific author with over 230 publications, Professor Dakanalis’s expertise spans the epidemiology and treatment of contemporary mental health issues. His passion for e-therapy and AI-driven mental health apps aligns perfectly with Rementes mission to democratize wellbeing.

Professor Dakanalis brings invaluable experience from his involvement with leading universities like Yale and Oxford and his contributions to nutritional and lifestyle psychiatry. His extensive accolades, including over 50 international awards, highlight his commitment to advancing mental health and wellbeing.

We are honored to have Professor Dakanalis on board to help us enhance our clinically validated, personalized wellbeing solutions, making mental health support accessible to everyone, anytime and anywhere.

Cookiepolicy

A cookie is a text file containing information stored on your computer or the device you use to visit a website. The cookie file usually contains the name of the website from which it comes and the lifespan (i.e. how long it will be stored on your device) as well as a value which is usually a randomly generated unique number.

Cookies are used to streamline the functionality associated with a website but also to collect information for the owner of the website. Cookies enable better customer adaptations on the website as it is possible to keep users apart from each other and thereby create a more tailored experience of the website. The cookie file is not harmful, cannot contain viruses or program code and cannot be linked to an individual or IP number.
